## 繁體中文

### 平台範圍:
- 單機 iOS simulator/local build，為後續擴展到 Android/WebGL 做準備。
- 支援 rewarded ads 優先且不破壞短局節奏。

- 玩家使用單手左右滑動控制角色閃避障礙物，體驗即時反饋與流暢操作。
- 60 FPS 下保持清楚 HUD、safe area、碰撞與重試流程，確保遊戲穩定與易上手。

### 技術限制:
- 使用 Unity 6000.0.71f1 開發，確保效能與跨平台支援。
- 為後續擴展到 Android/WebGL 做準備，保持開發流程一致性。

## English

### Platform Scope:
- Single-player iOS simulator/local build, preparing for future expansion to Android/WebGL.
- Prioritize rewarded ads without disrupting the short-session rhythm.

### Control Model:
- Players control the character by swiping left or right with one hand to dodge obstacles, experiencing immediate feedback and smooth operation.
- Maintain clear HUD, safe area, collision, and retry flow under 60 FPS, ensuring game stability and ease of use.

### Technical Constraints:
- Develop using Unity 6000.0.71f1 to ensure performance and cross-platform support.
- Prepare for future expansion to Android/WebGL, maintaining development workflow consistency.
