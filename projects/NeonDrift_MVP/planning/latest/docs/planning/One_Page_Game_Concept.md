## 繁體中文

### 產品願景:
- 創造一款能立即上手的快速漂移遊戲，提供直覺的操作體驗與令人上癮的遊戲循環。
- 打造一個重複性高、短時間內即可享受樂趣的遊戲，讓玩家在碎片時間中也能持續遊玩。

### 目標受眾:
- 主要針對13歲以上的手機休閒射擊遊戲愛好者，特別是喜歡快速反應遊戲與霓虹風格賽車畫面的玩家。
- 對於那些希望在短時間內體驗樂趣、不需要長時間投入的玩家有強烈吸引力。

### 核心價值:
- 一鍵觸控的漂移操作，配合清晰的反饋機制，讓遊戲簡單易學且充滿趣味。
- 簡潔明瞭的分數系統與失敗/重試循環，提升遊戲的重複可玩性。

### MVP 摘要:
- 在iOS平台上推出一款輕量級、可快速上手的漂移遊戲，並透過獎勵廣告實現商業化。
- 遊戲流程控制在一分鐘內，提供即時反饋與高分挑戰，吸引玩家持續遊玩。

## English

### Product Vision:
- Create a fast drifting game that players can pick up and enjoy immediately, with an intuitive control experience and addictive gameplay loop.
- Build a highly replayable game that offers fun in short bursts, allowing players to continue engaging during break times.

### Target Audience:
- Primarily targeting mobile casual arcade players aged 13+, especially those who enjoy quick reflex games and neon racing visuals.
- Strong appeal for players seeking immediate fun without long-term commitment.

### Core Value:
- One-tap drifting controls with clear feedback mechanisms, ensuring easy learning and engaging gameplay.
- A clean scoring system combined with failure/retry loops to enhance replayability.

### MVP Summary:
- Launch a lightweight, instantly playable drifting game on iOS, monetized through rewarded ads after proving gameplay quality.
- Game flow controlled within one minute, offering instant feedback and high-score challenges to encourage continued play.
