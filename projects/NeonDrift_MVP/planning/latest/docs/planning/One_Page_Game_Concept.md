## 繁體中文

### 產品願景:
- 提供快速、易於上手的單手持玩漂移遊戲體驗。
- 建立可重複使用的 Unity MVP 開發流程，專注於 iOS 首次發布。

### 目標受眾:
- 移動休閒與街機玩家，年齡 13 歲以上。
- 喜歡反射、即時衝突和分數競賽遊戲的通勤者與短暫休息玩家。

### 核心價值:
- 單手漂移控制搭配即時可讀反饋。
- 快速失敗與即時重試循環，創造緊湊且高衝突感的遊戲節奏。

### MVP 摘要:
- 玩家能在 30 秒內掌握遊戲基本操作並體驗到樂趣。
- MVP 版本能成功在 iOS 平台上發布，具備可擴展的架構以支持後續平台。

## English

### Product Vision:
- Deliver a fast, easy-to-pick-up single-handed drifting game experience.
- Establish a reusable Unity MVP development pipeline focused on iOS first release.

### Target Audience:
- Mobile casual and arcade players age 13+.
- Commuters and short-break players who like reflex, near-miss, and score-chasing games but dislike long tutorials or complex progression.

### Core Value:
- Single-handed drift controls with immediate readable feedback.
- Fast failure and instant retry loop to create a tight, high-conflict gameplay rhythm.

### MVP Summary:
- Players can grasp the core mechanics and have fun within 30 seconds.
- The MVP version successfully launches on iOS with an extensible architecture for future platforms.
