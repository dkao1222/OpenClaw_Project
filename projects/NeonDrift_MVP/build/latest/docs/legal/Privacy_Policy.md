# Privacy Policy

## 繁體中文

- 本文件是 `NeonDrift_MVP` 上架 Apple App Store 與 Google Play 前的隱私權政策草稿，正式發布前需由產品負責人與法律顧問確認。
- 本遊戲預期使用 Unity 製作，並可能使用廣告服務支援免費遊玩收益模式。
- 可能收集或由第三方廣告 SDK 處理的資料包含 Advertising Data、Device ID、Product Interaction、Diagnostics 與粗略技術資料。
- 資料用途包含 app functionality、analytics、advertising or marketing、fraud prevention、security 與 compliance。
- 若啟用個人化廣告，iOS 必須先顯示 App Tracking Transparency consent；Android / EU / UK / EEA 使用者必須依適用法律顯示 consent flow。
- 本遊戲不得在未揭露與未取得必要同意前啟用 tracking、個人化廣告或跨 app/site 廣告識別。

## English

- This document is a draft privacy policy for `NeonDrift_MVP` before Apple App Store and Google Play submission, and must be reviewed by the product owner and legal counsel before release.
- The game is expected to be built with Unity and may use advertising services to support a free-to-play revenue model.
- Data that may be collected or processed by third-party ad SDKs includes Advertising Data, Device ID, Product Interaction, Diagnostics, and coarse technical data.
- Purposes include app functionality, analytics, advertising or marketing, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.
- If personalized ads are enabled, iOS must present App Tracking Transparency consent first; Android / EU / UK / EEA users must receive the required consent flow under applicable law.
- The game must not enable tracking, personalized advertising, or cross-app/site advertising identifiers before proper disclosure and required consent.
