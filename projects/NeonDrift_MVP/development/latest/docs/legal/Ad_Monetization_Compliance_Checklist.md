# Ad Monetization Compliance Checklist

## 繁體中文

- Revenue model: free-to-play with rewarded ads and optional interstitial ads; forced ads should not block core safety, privacy, or consent flows.
- ATT: iOS personalized ads and IDFA access require ATT prompt before tracking.
- Consent: GDPR / UK / EEA and other applicable regions require consent before personalized ads or non-essential ad identifiers.
- rewarded ads: reward must be explicit, delivered only after ad completion callback, and not misrepresent odds or gameplay progression.
- Frequency cap: interstitial ads should be capped to avoid deceptive or disruptive UX.
- No fingerprinting: do not use device fingerprinting to bypass ATT or user consent.
