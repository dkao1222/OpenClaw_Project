# Creative Pillars

## 繁體中文

### 速度可讀性
- 所有危險、獎勵與路線提示都必須在高速移動中清楚辨識。
- 速度感不可犧牲核心路線、碰撞風險與分數回饋的判讀。

### 霓虹聚焦
- 視覺能量要服務賽道判讀，不可遮蔽玩家狀態或漂移時機。
- 色彩與動效優先強化速度、危險與成功回饋。

### 立即重試信心
- 失敗必須公平、節奏快速，並讓玩家知道下一次能改善什麼。
- 重試流程要短，避免玩家在失敗後失去動機。

## English

### Speed Readability
- Every hazard, reward, and route cue must remain readable at racing speed.
- Speed sensation must not hide the racing line, collision risk, or scoring feedback.

### Neon Focus
- Visual energy supports route reading and never hides player state or drift timing.
- Color and motion prioritize speed, danger, and success feedback.

### Instant Retry Confidence
- Failure must feel fair, fast, and clear about what the player can improve next.
- Retry flow must stay short so failure does not break motivation.
